## 示例2：二叉树遍历打卡海报

```
A educational daily check-in poster in notebook scrapbook style. Cream paper background with torn left edge and hole punches.

Section 2: Orange rounded rectangle header "二、中序遍历（左-根-右）". White rounded card below with light shadow. "先递归左子树，再访问根节点，最后递归右子树" with example: D→B→E→A→F→C.

Section 3: Orange rounded rectangle header "三、后序遍历（左-右-根）". White rounded card below with light shadow. "先递归左子树，再递归右子树，最后访问根节点" with example: D→E→B→F→C→A.

Section 4: Orange rounded rectangle header "四、层序遍历". White rounded card below with light shadow. "按层从上到下，从左到右访问" with example: A→B→C→D→E→F.

高频易错点 box: Lightbulb icon, orange dashed border, "中序遍历BST得到有序序列；前序遍历用于恢复二叉树".

一句话记忆 box: Target icon, green border, "前根左右，中左根右，后左右根，层序用队列".

Style: notebook scrapbook, educational, clear hierarchy, friendly for young students. Aspect ratio 3:4.
```

## 示例3：排序算法对比知识卡

```
A educational comparison card in notebook scrapbook style. Cream colored paper background with torn paper edges on the left side and hole punches.

Title "排序算法大比拼：谁最快？" in bold navy blue thick rounded Chinese font, with yellow hand-drawn underline stroke. Subtitle "CSP-J高频考点" in forest green.

Section 1: Orange rounded rectangle header "一、冒泡排序". White rounded card below with light shadow. Bubble icon in blue. "相邻比较交换，每轮冒出最大值" with complexity O(n²) in orange.

Section 2: Orange rounded rectangle header "二、选择排序". White rounded card below with light shadow. Pointer icon in green. "每轮选最小值放到前面" with complexity O(n²) in orange.

高频易错点 box: Lightbulb icon, orange dashed border, "快排最坏O(n²)，平均O(n log n)；冒泡和选择都是O(n²)但冒泡稳定".

一句话记忆 box: Target icon, green border, "冒泡选择O(n²)，快排平均O(n log n)".

Decorative elements: scattered stars ☆, small leaf doodles, open book at bottom, pencil accent.

```
